About me

My name is Nicholas Veselov and I'm an independent developer from St. Petersburg. I have been working in the IT industry for over 17 years. I specialize in the development of software, assets for Unity, games on Unity, as well as the development of industrial automation, devices and gadgets.
I have been interested in electrical engineering and programming since early childhood, so I received a technical education with a degree in radio mechanics in order to better understand electrical and device circuitry, the principles of troubleshooting. This knowledge has helped and continues to help me in the development of both various devices and software. I graduated from an educational institution with a red diploma.
At the moment, I am involved in various developments with various companies, with military and government agencies, and I am also conducting my own developments. This site just provides technical documentation for some of my public assets for Unity and more. Also, at the moment I am working on the development of several of my games, which are at varying degrees of readiness.
