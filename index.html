Developer Nick Veselov

Hello, my name is Nicholas Veselov, and I'm thrilled to introduce myself as an independent developer hailing from the beautiful city of St. Petersburg. With over 17 years of experience in the IT industry, I have dedicated my career to crafting exceptional software, creating assets for Unity, developing captivating games on the Unity platform, and delving into the fascinating realm of industrial automation, devices, and gadgets.
From a young age, I've been captivated by the wonders of electrical engineering and programming, which led me to pursue a technical education in radio mechanics. This educational journey equipped me with the profound knowledge of electrical systems, device circuitry, and the art of effective troubleshooting. This comprehensive understanding continues to guide me in the development of innovative devices and cutting-edge software solutions. I'm proud to say that I graduated with honors, earning a red diploma from my esteemed educational institution.
Presently, I am fortunate to be engaged in diverse projects with various companies, as well as pursuing my own independent endeavors. On this website, you'll find detailed technical documentation for some of my publicly available Unity assets and much more. Additionally, I'm currently pouring my passion and expertise into the development of several exciting games, each at different stages of completion.
Thank you for taking the time to learn a little about me and my journey. I'm excited to embark on new challenges, push the boundaries of technology, and create memorable experiences through my work.
