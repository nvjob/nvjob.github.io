#NVJOB Dynamic Sky (Component Settings)



Component Settings (#NVJOB / Dynamic Sky)



Sky Movement
• 2D Sky - scrolling the sky for 2D scenes.
• Rotate Speed - speed of rotation of the direction vector of the Albedo motion.
• Rotate Distance - duration of movement in the direction of the direction of movement of the Albedo.
• Player - To move the sky behind the player. X and Z axis only. If you have a small location, you do not have to move the Sky behind the player. But if you have a large location, to feel the infinity of the sky, use this option.
