One Night

This game is at an early stage of development.
It will be a small game, it will have a small town with several houses available for research, and a procedural forest.
One Night will be a mix of Astral movies, Silent Hill and Alan Wake games.
A small town located in a deep forest. A girl named Diana wakes up at night from a loud sound. She is completely alone in the room, her husband has disappeared somewhere. Diana walks to the window, and sees her husband going to the forest...
